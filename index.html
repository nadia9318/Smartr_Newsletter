Grammatical errors are a no-no

A Career Builder survey found that 77% of employers screen applications out based on typos or grammar errors. And guess what percentage of resumes have errors? 58%! And we haven't even gotten to information accuracy.

The good news? You can make it to the upper 42% just by double-checking your application for errors. Tips for catching mistakes:
